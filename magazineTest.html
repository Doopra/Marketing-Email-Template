Featured Movies
For relaxation, we’ve got movies to keep you entertained during the weekend.
Thor: Love and Thunder
Love and Thunder is a 2022 American superhero film based on Marvel Comics featuring the character Thor, produced by Marvel Studios. It is the sequel to Thor: Ragnarok and the 29th film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe and it's in cinemas
 
Beast
Beast is a 2022 American survival thriller film directed by Baltasar Kormákur from a screenplay by Ryan Engle, based on a story by Jaime Primak Sullivan. The film stars Idris Elba, Iyana Halley, Leah Sava Jeffries, and Sharlto Copley and it's on cinema
The Woman King
The Woman King is a historical epic drama film set in the 1820s. It tells the story of the Agojie, a military unit composed entirely of women that protected the Dahomey kingdom in West Africa between the 17th and 19th centuries. 
 
Meet Cute
Meet Cute is an American romantic comedy film starring Kaley Cuoco and Pete Davidson, and directed by Alex Lehmann. It was released on September 21, 2022, and it is available in cinemas.

The travel story of the week.

On September 1, 2022, Dubai’s Emirate Airlines announced on its website a plan to reinstate flights to/from Lagos. This change comes after the decision of the Central Bank of Nigeria to release a portion of the blocked funds the company wasn't able to repatriate from the country.
This is very welcome news as customers with previous bookings can get rebookings and refunds
